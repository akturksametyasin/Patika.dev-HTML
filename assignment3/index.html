CRISPY POTATO STICKS

  • 4-6 Persons
  • 15min Preparation, 20min Cooking

Ingridients

  • 2 potatoes (you can use 3-4 if they are small)
  • 3 tablespoons of starch (neither sweetcorn nor wheat doesn't matter)
  • Black pepper
  • Salt

For frying:

  • Liquid oil

Recipe:

  1. Potatoes are boiled and salt is added.

  2. Completely drained potatoes are mashed well.

  3. Add 3 tablespoons of starch and black pepper and knead.

  4. It is opened in medium thickness between two oiled papers.

  5. Long strips are cut.

  6. It is fried in hot oil. Enjoy your meal! üç¥
